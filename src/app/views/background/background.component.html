Background

I'm a Michigan-native who got married, packed my bags, and moved to Brooklyn, New York with my amazing wife, Deanna, and our senior Golden-Lab mix, Diesel. I graduated from Wayne State University in the Spring of 2016 with a Bachelor's Degree in Biomedical Engineering, but found myself working in a field that left me unfulfilled and unchallenged.

In the Summer of 2020, I made the decision to teach myself how to be a software developer and change careers. Starting with JavaScript, I immersed myself by picking up Angular, TypeScript, Node.js, and React over the course of the next year and a half while working full time as a Controls Engineer. By December 2021, I felt the fulfillment of starting a new career in a field where I could craft skills and products that I felt pride and passion as I found a spot on a wonderful team at Nexient.

Working at Nexient for over a year now, I have thrived by continuously learning from my mentors, honing my knowledge and tools, and raising my expectations and standards for how code should be written and tested. I believe that as a developer, the end experience for a user is what matters. As such, I constantly strive to ensure that the quality of work I produce is not only bug-free for release, but demonstrates high maintainability and best practices in the industry.
