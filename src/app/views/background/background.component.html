Background

I'm a Michigan-native who got married, packed my bags, and moved to Brooklyn, New York with my amazing wife and our energetic senior Golden-Lab pup. I graduated from Wayne State University with a Bachelor's Degree in Biomedical Engineering, but found myself working in a field that left me unfulfilled and unchallenged.

In time, I made the decision to teach myself how to be a software developer and change careers. Starting with JavaScript, I immersed myself by picking up Angular, TypeScript, Node.js, and React while working full time as a Controls Engineer. As proof of my motivation, I soon felt the fulfillment of starting a new career in a field where I could craft skills and products that I felt pride and passion in as I found a spot on a wonderful team at Nexient.

Working at Nexient, I have thrived by continuously learning from my mentors, honing my knowledge and tools, and raising my expectations and standards for how code should be written and tested. I believe that as a developer, the end experience for a user is what matters. As such, I constantly strive to ensure that the quality of work I produce is not only bug-free for release, but demonstrates high maintainability and best practices in the industry.
